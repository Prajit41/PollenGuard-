Understanding Pollen Allergies

Welcome to your comprehensive guide to understanding and managing pollen allergies.

What Causes Pollen Allergies?

Pollen allergies occur when your immune system mistakenly identifies pollen as a harmful substance.

Common Symptoms

🤧

Sneezing

👃

Runny Nose

👁️

Itchy Eyes

Pollen Types

🌳 Tree Pollen

Common in spring

🌾 Grass Pollen

Common in late spring and summer

🌼 Weed Pollen

Common in fall

Prevention Strategies

🌡️ Monitor Pollen Counts

Check daily pollen forecasts and stay indoors when counts are high.

🕶️ Wear Sunglasses

Protect your eyes from pollen when outdoors.

🚪 Keep Windows Closed

Use air conditioning instead of opening windows during high pollen seasons.
