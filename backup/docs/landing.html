What Are Pollen Allergies?

Pollen allergies, also known as hay fever or allergic rhinitis, occur when your immune system overreacts to pollen particles in the air.

Key Facts:

  • Affects 30% of adults and 40% of children
  • Most common in spring, summer, and fall
  • Symptoms include sneezing, runny nose, and itchy eyes
